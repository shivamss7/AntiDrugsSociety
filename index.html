About the Organization

The organization was founded in May 2017 by a group of young, enthusiastic and optimistic students who wished to see a change in the world. After that, the organization has skyrocketed through its works. We knew that it is going to be no walk on the cake for us to establish this society because there were no models of established society to be followed. Connections had to be made from scratch, members were to be selected and people to be made aware of its existence through its work and word of mouth. The very reason for the existence of this society is in its name "Anti-Drugs Society". The Society has received a very warm response from people from all over the Sub-Continent. This is what keeps us going all the time.

The society has the privilege to be an integral part of the database of United Nations Department of Economic and Social Affairs. The Society is also registered at United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime(UNDOC) prevention Hub. Narcotics Control Bureau (NCB) is committed to providing its expert guidance through the interaction of its senior functionaries with the members of the society. NCB, a Central Authority of Ministry of Home Affairs-Government of India is a nodal drug law enforcement and intelligence agency of India responsible for fighting drug trafficking and abuse of illegal substances. It has the unique distinction of being the only Anti-Drugs Society in India tackling the issue in universities/colleges on a large scale.

The society has the privilege of appointing delegates for the United Nations conferences, seminars, workshops and meetings. Only the member of this society has the authority of representing society at aforementioned events.
